How it works

Four tools, one rhythm.

Smart assessment upload

Drop in a photo, PDF, or screenshot of any task sheet and StudentSync reads the subject, due date, weighting and requirements.

Productivity dashboard

One screen for today's tasks, study sessions, and what's coming up — no more checking five different apps.
